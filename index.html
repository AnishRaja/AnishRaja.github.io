Similar correlation study



A similar study was conducted by Iowa State University in 1991 in which 605 students were surveyed. Their study suggested that there was a strong + correlation between grade level and difficulty of history courses.


Why Iowa State University’s data suggests a stronger correlation compared to Dublin Coffman High School’s data


- More students were surveyed

- There was an equal amount of students surveyed from each grade

- The data was analyzed and gathered in a more professional manner compared to the survey taken in Dublin Coffman High School




Mean



- Mean of course difficulty for freshman: 3.35 Median: 3.5

- Mean of course difficulty for sophomore: 6.75 Median: 7

- Mean of course difficulty for junior: 6.8 Median: 7

- Mean of course difficulty for senior: 5.3 Median: 5
